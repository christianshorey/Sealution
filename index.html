Sealution

Biodegradable sterile medical packaging made from seaweed-based biomaterials designed to replace single-use plastic in hospitals.

The Problem

Plastic Medical Waste

Hospitals rely heavily on disposable sterile plastic packaging for medical devices.

Few Sustainable Options

Most medical packaging today is petroleum-based and not biodegradable.

Growing Demand

Healthcare systems are actively searching for sustainable packaging alternatives.

Our Solution

Sealution develops biodegradable sterile peel pouches made from seaweed-derived biomaterials combined with medical-grade backing materials. Our packaging maintains clinical performance while reducing environmental waste.

How It Works

1. Seaweed Film

Renewable seaweed-based polymers form the primary packaging film.

2. Sterile Pouch Design

The film is laminated with medical-grade backing materials to create sterile pouches.

3. Testing & Validation

Packaging is tested for sterile barrier performance under ISO 11607 standards.
